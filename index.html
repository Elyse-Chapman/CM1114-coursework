Welcome!

Welcome to the RGU Book Club Society website!

No matter what you enjoy reading you can come join our meet-ups and make some new bookish friends. We meet at least once per week to discover and discuss new books, as well as just some quiet reading time!

We also sprinkle in some other events, such as film and quiz nights to change things up!
